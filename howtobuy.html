How to Buy AZC (PancakeSwap V3)

  1. Install MetaMask and switch to BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20).
  2. Fund your wallet with BNB for swaps and gas fees.
  3. Open PancakeSwap V3 and connect your wallet.
  4. Use this swap link: https://pancakeswap.finance/swap?outputCurrency=0xB9697BFC6eFA3e4893d0B1Ee75838eBE35AcedAd
  5. Enter BNB amount or AZC amount, approve token if prompted, then confirm the swap.
  6. After swap, add token to your MetaMask using contract address: 0xB9697BFC6eFA3e4893d0B1Ee75838eBE35AcedAd
